About Hazlin & Co

Hazlin & Co. is a boutique law firm situated in an affluent township in Petaling Jaya, Selangor, located within 10 km radius of major townships such as Mutiara Damansara, Bandar Sri Damansara, Taman Tun Dr. Ismail, Bandar Utama, Damansara Utama and Damansara Jaya. The Firm is established by a team of lawyers who are passionate about bringing justice to clients and upholding the law, providing a wide range of legal services accommodated to all kinds of legal issues, matters and disputes commonly faced by individuals, public and private sectors.

Meet Our Founder

Nurul Balqis binti Hazlin

Nurul Balqis binti Hazlin is the founder of Messrs. Hazlin & Co. She obtained her Bachelor of Laws (LLB) (Hons) from the International Islamic University Malaysia (IIUM) and was admitted as an Advocate and Solicitor of the High Court of Malaya in April 2019.

She commenced her legal career handling banking litigation, fraud cases, winding-up proceedings, intrusion onto land, and conveyancing matters. During her attachment and pupillage, she gained extensive experience in various areas of law including commercial litigation, debt recovery, and property transactions.

As founder of the firm, she handles various litigation matters including Commercial and Contractual Disputes, Debt Recovery, Family or Matrimonial Proceedings, Insurance matters, and other general litigation and corporate matters. Apart from litigation, she deals with conveyancing matters such as Property Transactions, applying for Letter of Administration and Grant of Probate, drafting of Tenancy Agreements, wills, and other comprehensive legal services tailored to client needs.
